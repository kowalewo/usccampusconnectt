About USC Campus Connect

Our Purpose and Mission

Welcome to USC Campus Connect, where being involved has never been easier!

Here you will be able to discover new campus events easier and quicker than ever! My goal is to help provide students with a one-stop platform to find and participate in events, engage on campus, and create a united community! The ultimate mission is to connect students and make sure everyone finds the club, organization, or activity where they fit in! So, whether you are looking to join a new club, volunteer around Columbia, or just have a group hangout sesh, this is the place where you can find it all!
